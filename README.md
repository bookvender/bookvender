## Overview

Many a times when we get promoted to next class or semester, or we get a job after preparing for any exam;
We have no idea what to do with these books. We generally sell our books to scarp dealers.
<br>
Here,
Book Vender serves as a platform where a person can sell his/her old/new books to the right person who wants that book.
<br>
You can share or donate your book to the needy.
And the buyer gets the book at very low cost.
<br>
Book Vender App helps to spread the flow of education.

- Feedback
